- #### KELENES

  Amino acid based chelating agents for use in cosmetic products (shampoos, bleaches, coloring, lotions, creams).

- #### LOWENOL STABILIZER L-536

Color additive used in hair color system to prevent undesirable surface oxidation, used mainly in light shades.

- #### LOWENOL STABILIZER D-552

  Color additive used in hair color system to prevent undesirable surface oxidation, used mainly in dark shades.
